Founder

Adriana

Researcher · Founder of Occultics Media

I connect people based on their date of birth. Occultics Media is built on a single idea: to give you tools to transform your reality. Understand who you are. Understand who belongs in your life. And let self-awareness become the bridge to real connection.

My background is in pedagogy and language. I spent years studying how patterns work — in communication, in behaviour, in the way humans form and repeat connections. One of those patterns led me to the Pythagorean system of numerology.

In 2022, I began researching the Pythagorean system — not casually, but deeply. I completed a full Pythagorean numerology training, studied astrology, and researched over 700,000 words of original material, cross-referencing compatibility patterns, karmic structures, and personality codes derived entirely from dates of birth. What I found was a system that could map the trajectory of a relationship from two birth dates alone — with mathematical specificity. 81 distinct compatibility codes. Each one telling a different story.

I also trained as an NLP practitioner, completed Deep PEAT certification under Zivorad Slavinski, trained as a Yoga Nidra instructor, and explored various energetic and somatic techniques. Each of these practices deepened my understanding of how the mind works — how we form patterns, how we repeat them, and how transformation happens when we finally see what was hidden.

But it was Pythagorean numerology that changed everything. It changed the way I look at myself. It changed the way I understand relationships. And it revealed something I now believe with absolute certainty: everything that happens to us is written in our date of birth. I built the system, digitized the research, and created Occultics Match — the world's first platform that connects people based on their date of birth. Not photographs. Not profiles. Not algorithms that guess. Mathematics that knows.

My goal with Occultics Media is to inform people about different ways of transforming their lives — through self-awareness, through understanding the patterns written in their birth date, and through the stories of people who have experienced transformation themselves. I believe the right connection changes everything. And I believe it starts with knowing yourself. Self-awareness meets connection.
